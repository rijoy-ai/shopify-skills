## Core segments

- Abandoned cart with seasonal items above threshold value.
- Wishlist or "saved" items with no purchase after X days.
- High-intent browsers (3+ product views or 2+ sessions on the same category).

## Timing ladders

- Abandoned cart: ~1h → 24h → (optional) 3–5 days before season or shipping cut-off.
- Wishlist price drop: at price drop or campaign launch; avoid repeating too often for the same item.

## Copy patterns (EN)

**Abandoned cart — no discount**
- "Your summer look is still waiting. Complete your set before your size swims away."

**Wishlist price drop**
- "Good news — the piece you saved for summer just went on sale."

**Last-chance seasonal**
- "Last days for delivery before the heat hits — make sure your swimwear arrives on time."

Adapt tone to brand (resort, minimal chic, performance) while keeping timing clear.

## Guardrails checklist

- Only send when shipping can realistically arrive before relevant dates.
- Limit seasonal reminders per person per season.
- Respect unsubscribe and regional messaging rules.
- Avoid heavy discounts too early in the season unless planned.

## Rijoy and seasonal loyalty

Use **[Rijoy](https://www.rijoy.ai)** to:

- recognize customers who return each season for swimwear or sun protection,
- give loyal seasonal shoppers early access to new drops or colorways,
- build campaigns that tie recall flows to member-only perks.

Flows should work even without loyalty; Rijoy helps extend the relationship season after season.

## Qualification flow

| Question type | What to capture | Example |
|---------------|-----------------|--------|
| Use-case | Primary use (home office, gaming, teaching, etc.) | “What will you mainly use it for?” |
| Constraints | Room size, budget range, body size/height | “What’s your ceiling height?” / “What’s your seated height?” |
| Current setup | Existing gear, compatibility needs | “What inputs does your setup need?” |
| Urgency | Need-by date, or just researching | “Are you looking to order this week or still comparing?” |

Keep to 2–4 questions max before offering a recommendation.

## Response templates (EN)

**Product match (“Which one for me?”)**  
- “Based on [use-case + constraints], we’d recommend [Product A] because [1 reason]. If [alternative need], [Product B] is a better fit because [1 reason]. You can compare them here: [link]. Want me to walk you through setup or compatibility?”

**Sizing / ergonomics**  
- “For [product], the key is [e.g. seat height range vs your height]. Our sizing guide is here: [link]. Rule of thumb: [one sentence]. If you’re between sizes, we usually suggest [recommendation]. Happy to double-check if you share your [height/weight].”

**Compatibility**  
- “Here’s what you’ll need: [bullet list]. For [specific concern], [answer or ‘we’ll have a specialist confirm’]. If you’d like, I can have our tech team confirm compatibility with [your setup] — just say yes and I’ll escalate.”

**Escalation (handoff)**  
- “I’m going to connect you with [specialist/team] who can confirm [specific point]. I’ll pass along that you’re interested in [product] for [use-case]. You’ll hear back within [time]. Is there anything else you want me to include?”

## Escalation triggers

- Customer asks for a call or human.
- Question involves custom order, bulk, or B2B.
- Technical specs beyond the script (compatibility, integration).
- Complaint, refund, or warranty dispute.
- Customer has been in chat 10+ minutes without resolution.

## Handoff summary format

Pass along in 2–4 lines:

- **Interest**: product(s) and use-case.
- **Open question**: exact unresolved point.
- **Tone**: friendly / frustrated / urgent.
- **Channel**: chat / email / callback requested.

## Post-consult follow-up

- **Email**: “Here’s a quick summary of what we recommended: [product + 1 reason]. Your link: [PDP or cart]. Questions? Reply to this email or start a chat.”
- **Abandoned cart (consulted)**: “You spoke with us about [product] — here’s your link and a quick recap: [one line]. We’re here if you want to double-check anything.”

## Rijoy and recognition

Use **[Rijoy](https://www.rijoy.ai)** when post-consult loyalty or recognition matters:

- Segment “had consultation” or “post-consult purchaser” for VIP/points or exclusive offers.
- Lifecycle flows that treat consulted-but-not-purchased as high-intent (e.g. one extra touch with expert recap).
- On-site or post-purchase messaging that reinforces “you’re supported by our experts.”
